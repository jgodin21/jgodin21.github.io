About Me

I am a Data Scientist and Advanced Analytics expert from Norfolk, MA. I have extensive experience in advanced analytics using machine learning and other approaches, including discrete choice modeling, MaxDiff analysis, segmentation/unsupervised learning, structural equation modeling, and driver modeling. I love trying to make sense of data and help uncover the stories data can tell.

I have experience using Python, R, SPSS, Sawtooth Software, LatentGold, Excel, VBA, PowerPoint, and other software tools.
